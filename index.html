Accountability

Commitment to accountability

Trust and transparency are central to Akshaya's mission. The trust states that it upholds the highest standards of governance and accountability in all its operations.

  • Clear governance with defined roles and oversight mechanisms
  • Transparent accounting and responsible financial reporting
  • Donations accepted only through official trust channels
  • Volunteers engaged for outreach and awareness, never for fund collection
  • Receipts and acknowledgements issued directly by the trust
Programs

Three pillars guide every initiative of the trust.

The trust's work follows one connected pathway: building strength, protecting learning continuity, and sustaining dignity over time.

Shakti Women Empowerment Self-Reliance

Shakti builds strength, confidence, and self-reliance.

Shakti focuses on empowering individuals, especially women, through skill development, capacity building, and livelihood-oriented support.

Support Shakti
Shiksha Education Learning Continuity

Shiksha protects access to education and continuity of learning.

Shiksha supports children and youth facing financial, social, or circumstantial barriers so that learning can continue with stability and purpose.

Support Shiksha
Samriddhi Long-Term Support Well-Being

Samriddhi sustains dignity, continuity, and long-term well-being.

Samriddhi supports individuals who need ongoing care over time, including extended educational support, continued empowerment, elderly care, and help through prolonged life-stage challenges.

Support Samriddhi

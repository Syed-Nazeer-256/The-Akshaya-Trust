Our Story

Founded in 2013, rooted in purpose, guided by three pillars.

Akshaya Charitable Trust was established by Saradhamma K with a clear mission: to support empowerment, education, and sustained well-being for underserved communities in Bangalore and beyond. Registered as a public charitable trust (Reg. No. GAN-4-00049-2013-14), Akshaya operates through Shakti, Shiksha, and Samriddhi — three interconnected pillars that guide every initiative.

✦

Mission

To empower individuals and families through sustainable programs that build capacity, protect education, and promote long-term stability — delivered with transparency, dignity, and accountability.

◇

Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of circumstance, has access to empowerment, education, and the foundation for sustained well-being and self-reliance.

❖

Values

Transparency in governance, continuity across all initiatives, dignity in every interaction, and direct accountability to every contributor and stakeholder.

Frequently Asked

Common questions about Akshaya Charitable Trust.

Are donations tax-deductible?+

Yes. Akshaya Charitable Trust holds a valid 80G registration (AALTA6108JF20251). All donations are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Indian Income Tax Act. An official receipt is issued for every contribution.

How are funds used?+

Funds are directed towards three pillars — Shakti (empowerment), Shiksha (education), and Samriddhi (sustained well-being). The trust maintains audited financial statements and provides direct accountability for all contributions.

How can I verify the trust?+

You can verify Akshaya through its registration (GAN-4-00049-2013-14), PAN (AALTA6108J), NGO Darpan (KA/2025/0657083), or by contacting us directly at 9844834103 or hope.akshaya@gmail.com.

Can I volunteer remotely?+

Yes. While many of our programs are Bangalore-based, we welcome remote volunteers who can contribute through content creation, mentoring, digital support, and awareness campaigns.

Does Akshaya accept CSR contributions?+

Absolutely. Akshaya holds a valid CSR Registration (CSR00099254) and is equipped to provide all compliance documentation required for corporate CSR partnerships.

How does the trust ensure accountability?+

Through direct acknowledgement for every contribution, audited annual financial statements, clear governance structures, and maintaining full statutory compliance including 12A, 80G, and NGO Darpan registrations.

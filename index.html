Three Pillars of Hope.

Strength, Education, and Prosperity — the foundations upon which Akshaya builds futures.

Shiksha — Education

Learning

Full-spectrum digital literacy and academic scholarships for 8,420 first-generation rural students. We cover fees, books, uniforms, meals, and mentorship from Class 1 through graduation.

Shakti — Strength

Vitality

15 surgical centers and 42 mobile diagnostic labs across 6 states, providing free cataract surgeries, cardiac screenings, and maternal care to 3,600+ patients annually.

Samriddhi — Prosperity

Resilience

Climate-resilient toolkits, micro-capital, and cooperative market linkages for 4,200 smallholder farming families. Average income increased by 340% within 3 years.

our philosophy

Radical Stewardship.

Akshaya Charitable Trust was founded in 2011 on one premise: charity must be an investment in human flourishing, not a transaction. We deploy a data-led, impact-verified model that traces every rupee from donor to beneficiary.

Our Board of Governors includes former IAS officers, healthcare professionals, and rural development economists ensuring zero tolerance for overhead waste.

Vision

A rural India where every citizen is health-secure, digitally literate, and economically sovereign.

Mission

Convert high-intent philanthropy into auditable, life-changing outcomes through precision governance.

Values

Integrity-first. Community-led. Data-verified. Zero overhead tolerance.
